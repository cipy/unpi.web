# Welcome to unPi Apps Support

This is the support center for all applications developed by **Asociația unPi pentru Școlari** — a non-profit organization bringing quality digital tools to Romanian students.

If you're experiencing an issue, have a question, or want to suggest an improvement, the best way to reach us is through our **[GitHub Issues tracker](https://github.com/unPi-ro/support/issues)**. It's free, open, and lets us keep track of every request.

[Open a support ticket](https://github.com/unPi-ro/support/issues/new){: .btn} &nbsp; [Browse existing issues](https://github.com/unPi-ro/support/issues){: .btn}

---

## Help Topics

### 🐛 Report a Bug

Found something that isn't working as expected? Please let us know by [opening a bug report](https://github.com/unPi-ro/support/issues/new?labels=bug). Include the app name, your device type, and the steps to reproduce the problem.

### 💡 Feature Request

Have an idea that would make our apps more useful for students or teachers? [Submit a feature request](https://github.com/unPi-ro/support/issues/new?labels=enhancement) and tell us what you'd like to see.

### ❓ General Question

Not sure how something works, or need help getting started? [Ask a question](https://github.com/unPi-ro/support/issues/new?labels=question) and our team will get back to you.

### 🔒 Privacy & Data

Questions about how our apps handle student data? We take privacy seriously and comply with Romanian and EU regulations (GDPR). [Contact us about privacy](https://github.com/unPi-ro/support/issues/new?labels=privacy).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I submit a support request?**
Click the **Open a support ticket** button above. You'll need a free GitHub account. Describe your issue as clearly as possible — which app, which device, and what happened. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

**Which apps does this support page cover?**
All applications published by Asociația unPi pentru Școlari. If you're unsure whether an app is ours, check the publisher name in your app store or school portal.

**Is my child's data safe?**
Yes. Our apps do not collect personally identifiable information without explicit consent, and all data is handled in compliance with GDPR. For specific concerns, open a Privacy & Data issue.

**Do I need a GitHub account to get support?**
Yes, a free [GitHub account](https://github.com/join) is required to open an issue. If that's a barrier, you can reach us via the email listed on our [GitHub organization page](https://github.com/unPi-ro).

**Can I request an app for my school?**
Absolutely! Open a Feature Request and describe what you need — include the grade level, subject, and any specific requirements. We love hearing from teachers and school staff.

**How long does it take to get a response?**
We typically respond within 1–2 business days. Complex issues may take longer, but we'll keep you updated directly in the issue thread.
